## 1. Purpose & scope
The canonical reference for the **36 customer-facing domain terms** the platform uses, so every surface defines a term the same way. The **authoritative wording** is the `Glossary.xlsx` entry; the definitions below are the standard industry meanings to keep usage consistent and are **not** a license to diverge from the spreadsheet. Adding or changing a term follows `faq-and-glossary-governance.md` (content review + SME review for technical terms).

## 2. Governing rules (MUST)
- A term's definition is **identical** on every surface (blog, FAQ, product, tooltips).
- Technical/methodology terms (EUR, decline, allocation, acre/well) match the implementation and stay **hedged** (estimates, not actuals).
- Terms link from content where they first appear (`seo-governance.md` internal-linking).
- Canonical wording lives in `Glossary.xlsx`; this file mirrors it for governance, not replaces it.

## 3. Core ownership & interest terms (confirmed from the glossary)
| Term | Standard meaning (canonical wording in `Glossary.xlsx`) |
|---|---|
| Mineral Interest | Ownership of the minerals beneath a tract, including the right to extract or lease them. |
| Royalty Interest | A share of production revenue free of production costs, paid to the mineral owner. |
| Net Revenue Interest (NRI) | The owner's share of production revenue after royalties/burdens. |
| Working Interest | The operating interest that bears a share of costs. |
| Net Mineral Acres (NMA) | The owner's net acreage in a tract after applying their fractional interest. |
| Executive Rights | The right to lease the minerals and negotiate lease terms. |
| Decimal Interest | The fractional ownership expressed as a decimal, used to compute an owner's share. |

## 4. Lease & production terms (confirmed)
| Term | Standard meaning |
|---|---|
| Held By Production (HBP) | A lease kept in force as long as the well produces in paying quantities. |
| Lease Bonus | An up-front payment to the owner for signing a lease. |
| Division Order | A document confirming each owner's decimal interest for revenue distribution. |
| Pooling / Unitization | Combining tracts/leases into a single unit for development. |
| Stripper Well | A low-rate well at or below the RRC stripper threshold. |
| Allocation Well | A well producing from multiple leases/tracts with production allocated among them. |

## 5. Measurement & estimate terms (confirmed)
| Term | Standard meaning |
|---|---|
| EUR (Estimated Ultimate Recovery) | Produced volume + remaining estimated reserves; on the platform shown over a "next 6 years" window. A **modeled estimate**. |
| MCF | One thousand cubic feet of gas. |
| BOE | Barrel of oil equivalent (gas converted to oil-equivalent volume). |
| MVestimate | The platform's modeled **projected six-year earnings** estimate (see `mvestimate-governance.md`). A modeled estimate, not a guaranteed value. |
| Decline Curve | The modeled production decline used to forecast future volumes (Arps; see `decline-curve-methodology-governance.md`). |
| Acre/Well | Spatial density measure (8,000-ft lateral reference) used in valuation context. |

## 7. Usage examples
- **Good:** "Your Net Revenue Interest (NRI) is your share of revenue after royalties — see the glossary." (term linked, consistent definition)
- **Bad:** redefining NRI differently on a blog post than in the glossary, or presenting EUR/MVestimate without the "estimate" hedge.

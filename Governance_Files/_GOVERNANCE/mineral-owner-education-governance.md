## 1. Purpose & scope
Govern owner-education content so it **empowers without advising** — the heart of the brand's "Empowerment" value and constitution P2.

## 2. Principles (MUST)
Educate on what owners own and how to read their data; **must not** tell them to lease, sell, or sign. Every decision-adjacent page carries "educational, not advice; verify independently."

## 3. Required elements
One H1; plain language; glossary links for jargon; provenance for any figure; a clear next step (claim / learn more). Estimates labeled.

## 4. Good vs bad
| Good | Bad |
|---|---|
| "How to read your Net Revenue Interest." | "Why you should sell your minerals now." |
| "What an HBP lease means for you." | "The best operators to lease with." |

## 5. QA checklist
☐ Plain language ☐ Glossary-linked ☐ No advice/steering ☐ Provenance ☐ Clear next step ☐ Estimates labeled.

## 6. Anti-patterns
Directive language; operator steering; jargon without glossary links; figures without provenance.

## 7. Evidence notes & gaps
Grounded in `Blog.xlsx` compliance findings + glossary.

## Deep context (2026-06-30) — education surfaces & standards

Education is Mineral View's top-of-funnel and trust-builder. Governed surfaces (grounded in the content/SEO/video work summaries):

- **Glossary:** clear, accurate definitions of oil & gas terminology (mineral rights, royalty/NRI/NMA, HBP, EUR, decimal interest, division order, RRC, API-42, etc.), with supporting images/HTML, optimized for understanding and search. Built on a shared **glossary content framework**.
- **Blog (25+ articles):** SEO-optimized, intent-mapped articles for owners, royalty owners, operators, and investors; reviewed/QA'd before publish.
- **Video (YouTube):** educational long- and short-form content (e.g. "Texas Minerals 101", "Things Nobody Tells Texas Mineral Owners") combining motion graphics and plain-language explanation.
- **FAQ & in-product education:** tooltips, microcopy, and guidance that teach as the user works.

**Standards (MUST):** explain to a **non-expert owner** without dumbing down the facts; use **canonical glossary terms** consistently (`MineralView_Glossary.md`, `texas-oil-and-gas-domain-governance.md`); **Texas-scoped** claims only (P1) — US/educational framing must not imply nationwide platform data; **no overstatement** of what the platform proves (P2); cite RRC where credibility matters. Content aligns to brand voice (`Customer_Communication_Style_Guide.md`) and passes the content QA gate before publish.

## 1. Purpose & scope
Govern testimonials, reviews, and social proof — authenticity, attribution, claim sensitivity, and placement. Testimonials are persuasive **and** risky: a fabricated quote or one that implies a guaranteed financial outcome is both a trust breach and a claim exposure. Mineral View's audience is scam-wary, so authentic, modest social proof outperforms inflated claims.

## 2. Rules (MUST)
- Testimonials are **real and verifiable**; **do not** fabricate, embellish, or composite.
- Attribute accurately (name/role as permitted) and honor consent.
- A testimonial **must not** carry a claim the platform itself couldn't make — guaranteed value/returns, advice, or nationwide coverage.
- Edits for length/clarity **must not** change meaning.
- Where outcomes are implied, add "results may vary"; don't overstate representativeness.

## 3. Placement
Place near the relevant value proposition; don't cluster claim-heavy testimonials on pricing/value pages where they amplify a value claim.

## 4. Workflow
Source → verify + consent → claim-check (`claim-risk-register.md`) → place → review on cadence.

## 5. QA checklist
☐ Real + consented ☐ Accurate attribution ☐ No prohibited claims ☐ Meaning preserved ☐ "Results may vary" where outcomes implied ☐ Current.

## 6. Anti-patterns
Fabricated/embellished/composite quotes; implied guarantees; outcome claims without "results may vary"; using a testimonial to make a claim the brand legally can't.

## 7. Evidence notes & gaps
Testimonials are confirmed present on the contact/testimonials page; the verification status of each is **Not confirmed from the uploaded files** and must be checked before use.

## Deep context (2026-06-30) — truthful social proof

Testimonials, reviews, and social proof build trust — and are a **legal/claims risk** if misused.

**Rules (MUST):**
- **Real & consented:** only genuine testimonials from real users with **permission** to publish their words/name; no fabricated or composite quotes presented as real.
- **No outcome promises:** a testimonial must not become an implied guarantee of returns/results; estimates and individual experiences are not representative claims (`Compliance_And_Disclaimers.md`, P2/P3).
- **Accuracy:** any figures inside a testimonial follow the same provenance/vintage/labeled-estimate rules as the rest of the platform.
- **Attribution & updates:** attribute accurately; remove on request/withdrawal of consent; keep a record of consent.
- **Review:** social-proof content claiming results is **legal-reviewed** before publish and passes the publish gate.

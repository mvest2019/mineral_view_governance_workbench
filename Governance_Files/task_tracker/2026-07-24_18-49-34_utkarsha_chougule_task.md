**Regression Testing of Auto-Debit, Yearly Subscription Plans, and Plan Upgrade/Downgrade Flows**

**Task Description**

Performed comprehensive regression and functional testing of the subscription management module, focusing on the newly introduced **Yearly subscription plans**, **Auto-Debit functionality**, and **plan upgrade/downgrade workflows**. Validated subscription lifecycle behavior across the application, payment gateway (Braintree), and Manage Plan section to ensure accurate billing, plan transitions, and UI consistency.

During testing, identified and reported multiple functional and UI issues, including:

- Verified Auto-Debit functionality for both Monthly and Yearly subscription plans.

- Verified that the **Next Billing Date** for Monthly subscriptions is incorrectly displayed as a Yearly renewal date.
- Validated subscription status, effective dates, renewal dates, billing information, and plan availability across the application.
- Reported UI/UX inconsistencies and functional defects related to subscription management, ensuring correct business rule implementation for Yearly and Monthly plans.

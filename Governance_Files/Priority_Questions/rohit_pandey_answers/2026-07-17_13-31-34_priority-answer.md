Question:
If the landing page is to show real platform statistics instead of marketing copy, who verifies those figures and how often are they refreshed?

Answer:
The figures come straight from source-of-truth MongoDB queries — every number traces to a specific collection and field, so verification is mechanical: anyone can re-run the documented queries and reproduce them. No owner is formally assigned yet; the natural fit is whoever runs the data pipeline. Since we chose hardcoded copy over the self-updating endpoint to ship fast, refresh is manual — re-query when numbers move meaningfully, roughly quarterly. One caveat: "updated daily" holds for the filings feed, but decline curves (May 2026) and allocation data refresh slower, so that claim should be scoped to filings before publishing.

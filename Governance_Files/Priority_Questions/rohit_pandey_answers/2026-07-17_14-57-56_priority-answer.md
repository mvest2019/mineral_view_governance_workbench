# Priority Question Answer

Question:
Is the correct county coverage 196 or 161, and which system is the authoritative source for that number?

Answer:
161 is correct and should stay — my earlier advice to change it to 196 was wrong. The 196 is a raw distinct count of the county_upper field, inflated by a literal "NULL" value, offshore blocks (BRAZOS-SB, HIGH IS-LB), and counties with almost no data (Baylor and San Patricio have 1 record each). Filtering to counties with ≥1,000 owner records yields 157 — very close to 161 — which suggests 161 is a deliberately curated coverage figure, not an outdated one. MongoDB is authoritative for the raw data but not for the coverage claim, since the "what counts as covered" rule lives outside the database and I couldn't locate or verify it — so whoever produced 161 owns that number until the rule is documented.

# Priority Question Answer

Question:
What is our policy for AI-generated diagrams published on the site, and does it require any disclosure?

Answer:
When publishing AI-generated diagrams, we first gather information from reliable and authoritative sources. The underlying data and concepts are verified for accuracy and relevance, and we use AI only to help visualize that verified information in a clear and user-friendly format.

Before publication, each diagram is manually reviewed to ensure it accurately represents the underlying data, is consistent with the content on the page, and does not introduce factual errors or misleading information.

At present, we do not include a disclosure indicating that the diagrams were created with AI. The focus is on ensuring the diagrams are factually accurate, visually clear, and supported by verified information before they are published.

# Priority Question Answer

Question:
Are campaign recipient lists checked against opt-outs and unsubscribe consent before a bulk campaign sends?

Answer:
Opt-outs are enforced — but at send time, not at list-build time. sendViaGraph (graphMail.ts:69) calls suppressionFor(email) and throws "Blocked" and never sends if the address is UNSUBSCRIBED or BOUNCED (from email_verifications).
So a suppressed recipient can be added to a scheduled batch, but is stopped at the moment of sending — no email goes out.

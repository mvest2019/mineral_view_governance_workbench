# Priority Question Answer

Question:
Do the randomized send intervals risk pushing time-sensitive emails past their corrected scheduled time?

Answer:
Only the first email lands exactly on time; each later one drifts by cumulative random gaps, so later emails in a batch send progressively later. Fine for outreach, not ideal for time-critical sends — use a small/zero stagger for those.

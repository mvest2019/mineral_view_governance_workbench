# Priority Question Answer

Question:
What is the definition and calculation method behind Reply Rate and First Reply Time, and who verified them?

Answer:
Reply Rate = replied ÷ sent × 100, rounded to 0.1% (campaignStats.ts:192). It's null until at least one email has sent. Denominator is sent, not recipients.
"Replied" = a recipient has an inbound email (an actions row, direction INBOUND) with a timestamp at or after their send instant. One reply per recipient is counted.
First Reply Time (firstReplyMinutes, campaignStats.ts:193) = the fastest single reply in the campaign = min(reply_time − send_time) in minutes (floored at 0). There's also a medianReplyMinutes.
Verification: these are computed deterministically in code from scheduled_emails + inbound actions; they were validated by build/type-checks and code review at ship time — there was no separate formal sign-off/QA owner. (Honest gap: no independent verifier on record.)

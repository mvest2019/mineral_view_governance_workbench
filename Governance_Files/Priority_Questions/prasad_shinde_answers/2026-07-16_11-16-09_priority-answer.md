Question:
Why is the email dispatcher scheduled on the staging server, and is staging sending real emails to live members?

Answer:
The 108 "staging" box is only used as an always-on machine to trigger the production dispatcher every 5 min (Vercel Hobby can't run a 5-min cron). The actual sending runs on production (Vercel + prod DB), so yes, real emails reach live members — but the staging server is just the alarm clock, not the sender.

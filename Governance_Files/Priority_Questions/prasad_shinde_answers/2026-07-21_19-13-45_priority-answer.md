# Priority Question Answer

Question:
Does safe campaign deletion also remove the underlying sent-email and metrics history, and what is the retention policy?

Answer:
Deleting a campaign (campaigns/[id]/route.ts:60):

Cancels any not-yet-sent emails (SCHEDULED/SENDING → CANCELLED) so they never fire.
Deletes the Campaign row; click & open tracking events cascade-delete (onDelete: Cascade) — so that tracking history is removed.
Sent emails are preserved — their campaign_id is set to null (onDelete: SetNull), so the sent records + conversation/thread history stay intact (just ungrouped). Reply/bounce/opt-out data lives in actions/email_verifications and is untouched.
Retention policy: none formal/automated — deletion is manual and permanent for the grouping + tracking events; sent history is retained indefinitely.

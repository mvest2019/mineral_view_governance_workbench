# Priority Question Answer

Question:
How is "automatic campaign grouping" defined, and can it retroactively regroup or mis-attribute previously sent emails?

Answer:
A campaign = one bulk/scheduled batch. It's created once, at schedule time (schedule-bulk/route.ts:128): a Campaign row is made and every scheduled_email in that batch is stamped with its campaign_id.
Membership is fixed at creation and never recomputed — metrics are derived at read time from that stored campaign_id. So it cannot retroactively regroup or re-attribute already-sent emails.
Ad-hoc/legacy emails have campaign_id = null and are simply excluded. No mis-attribution risk.

# Priority Question Answer

Question:
Who approves social and YouTube content before publishing, and does it carry the no-investment-advice disclaimer?

Answer:
Approval: Content is cross-checked across GPT, Claude, and Gemini — comparing their responses on the drafts — and then we review and approve it ourselves before it goes live. So it's a multi-model check plus our own final sign-off (a self-review process, not a separate external/formal approver).

Disclaimer: The content is built with compliance in mind — the BOLD metals format has compliance rules baked in, and CTAs default to engagement rather than pushing a financial action. So anything touching prices, ratios, or anything that could read as investment guidance should carry a short "for information only, not financial advice" line before going live; purely brand/educational posts with no numbers are lower-risk.

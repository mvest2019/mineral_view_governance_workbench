# Priority Question Answer

Question:
Does a human approve AI-drafted CRM emails before they are sent to real users?

Answer:
Yes. Every AI-drafted email is reviewed and approved by a person before it goes to a real user — nothing sends automatically.

The models draft and suggest wording; they have no send authority. We check the output, verify the tokens resolve to correct values, and make the final call ourselves. The AI is a drafting tool, not an approver.

Question:
Is there any alerting for scans that hang on a Gemini timeout, or was this one caught by chance?

Answer:
No automated alerting. The scan engine has fallback logic — Gemini-primary with an OpenAI fallback, plus per-source fetch budgets so slow feeds don't stall everything — but that's built-in resilience, not alerting. Nothing fires a notification when a scan hangs or a provider times out.

So this one was caught by manual review, not by chance exactly but by us noticing and digging in — the same way the OpenAI key-shadow issue was found by inspecting it rather than being flagged automatically.

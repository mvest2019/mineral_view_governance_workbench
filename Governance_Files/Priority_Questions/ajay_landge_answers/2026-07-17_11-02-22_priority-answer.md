# Priority Question Answer

Question:
What is "Trendelier," and do its scan outputs feed user-facing data or only internal content research?

Answer:
Trendelier is our own internal content tool. It scans sources (news feeds, YouTube trending, structured data, etc.) per channel, then generates insights and content drafts — the reel/short-form scripts, long-form YouTube scripts, CTAs — and also tracks social analytics for our channels. Basically it powers our content research and planning across the different brand channels.

On the scan outputs: they're internal research/planning outputs — they feed our own content creation (insights and draft suggestions we act on), not any user-facing product or public data. The only thing that ends up user-facing is the finished content we choose to publish to the brand accounts after our own review; the raw scan outputs themselves stay internal.

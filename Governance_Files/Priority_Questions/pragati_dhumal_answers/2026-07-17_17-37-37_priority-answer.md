# Priority Question Answer

Question:
Which navigation items are proposed for removal as "redundant," and who approves losing those entry points?

Answer:
The recommendations identify two navigation items as potentially redundant:

"Resource Library" within the Resources dropdown, because the dropdown itself already functions as the resource library and directly lists the same destinations.
"Explore the Map" within the Resources dropdown, but only if it is promoted to a dedicated top-level navigation item. In that case, the duplicate dropdown entry could be removed to avoid presenting the same destination twice.

These are recommendations intended to simplify navigation without reducing access to any content. They are not implementation decisions. Any changes to the site's information architecture or navigation should be reviewed and approved by the appropriate product owner, UX/design lead, or business stakeholders to ensure they align with business priorities, user needs, and content strategy before being implemented.

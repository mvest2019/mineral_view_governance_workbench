# Priority Question Answer

Question:
Does the multi-tranche undercount also affect the MVestimate values owners see in the live portal, not just the dossier tool?

Answer:
No. The undercount is only in the raw claim record (membersclaimedleases), which stores a single tranche. The live portal reads the owner records and sums the tranches, so owners already see the full, correct value on login. We verified this — our summed value for Farmar matched the portal to the dollar. So the portal is not undercounted; the fix was making our tool sum the same way. (Separate note: the duplicate-2024-CAD rows do the opposite — they over-count and can exceed 100% on the portal; that's the issue awaiting the 2025 data.)

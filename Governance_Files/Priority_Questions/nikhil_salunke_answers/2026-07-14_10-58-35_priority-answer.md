What is the authoritative State Code → property-classification mapping, and where is it documented?

These codes are used to identify land and property types associated with oil and gas mineral interests and are referenced during the data extraction, validation, and classification process. The mapping is documented within our internal data processing documentation and classification logic to ensure consistent interpretation of property descriptions across the platform.

Before replacing the live marketing site, who validates URL, redirect, and SEO parity to prevent traffic regressions?

Answer:
The Product Owner, Development Team, and Marketing/SEO team should jointly validate URL structure, redirects (301), metadata, and SEO parity before replacing the live site.
